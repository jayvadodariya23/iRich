An initiative to promote UNESCO World Heritage Sites of St Maarten

We want to educate people around the world about the heritage sites of St Maarten and its important cultural significance which displays its magnificent history in different forms (tangible, intangible or natural). As of now, there are a total of 18 UNESCO World Heritage sites.
